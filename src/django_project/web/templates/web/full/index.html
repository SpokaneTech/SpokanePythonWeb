A vibrant community of Python enthusiasts, learners, and professionals. Join us for engaging talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Join Us

Our Mission and Values

As a local chapter of the Python Software Foundation, we're dedicated to promoting, protecting, and advancing the Python programming language and supporting the growth of a diverse community of Python programmers.

Community First

We believe in building lasting connections and fostering an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome, regardless of their experience level or background.

Continuous Learning

We're committed to sharing knowledge through talks, workshops, code reviews, and mentorship opportunities that help everyone level up their Python skills.

Collaboration & Innovation

We encourage collaboration on open source projects, community initiatives, and real-world problems that benefit both our local community and the broader Python ecosystem.

Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants.

Our Code of Conduct follows the Python Software Foundation's Code of Conduct, which ensures our community remains welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.
